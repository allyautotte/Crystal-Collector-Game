Crystal Collectors


You will be given a random number below.

There are four crystals and each crystal has a different value.

Discover their values by clicking on the crystals.

Try to match your total score to the random number.

Choose wisely! If your score goes above the random number, you lose.

Each time the game starts, the values of each crystal change too.


Good Luck!
